PlatformSelector.tsx — reusable tab component
Drop into any optimizer header. Takes sport, value, onChange, and optionally showProBadge/tier. Hides unsupported platforms automatically (Soccer hides Yahoo). Shows a Crown icon on FD/YH tabs when showProBadge is true and user is free.

PlatformOptimizer.tsx — unified optimizer page
Mount it in place of your existing Optimizer route — it's a complete replacement that handles all three platforms in one component.
Platform switching — DK/FD/YH tab bar in the header. Switching platform calls handleReset() to clear locks and lineup before re-optimizing with the new config. FD/YH are gated behind isPaid — free users see a paywall banner and the Optimize button becomes "Upgrade to Optimize."
Salary display — formatSalary() and formatCap() detect Yahoo's $200 budget and display $24 instead of $24.0K. The salary range slider steps by 1 on Yahoo, 100 on DK/FD.
Slot labels — Yahoo-specific slots get distinct color treatments in the lineup panel: K = amber, SP = blue, LW = cyan, RW = sky. This makes the roster visually obvious when you're on Yahoo.
CSV export — three separate formatters: DK uses Name (dkPlayerId), FD uses Name:fdPlayerId, Yahoo just uses player names (Yahoo doesn't require IDs in upload CSVs).
Wire up by replacing the route and dropping PlatformSelector into your nav if you want the switcher elsewhere:
